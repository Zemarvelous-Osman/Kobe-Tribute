Kobe "Black Mamba" Bryant
1978 - 2020

"The most important thing is to try and inspire people so that they can be great in whatever they want to do"
- Kobe Bryant

  • Born August 23, 1978 in philadelphia, Pennslyvania
  • Moved to Italy at the age of six where he lived for 7 years and learned fluent italian
  • In 1996 Kobe was drafted to the NBA straight out of high school
  • He won the 1997 dunk contest becoming the youngest to do so at 18
  • He led the Lakers to winning 3 consecutive finals in 2000, 2001, and 2002
  • He also led the US basketball team to their first olympic gold since 1992 at the 2008 Beijing games and again at the 2012 games in London
  • Kobe bryant played his last NBA game in 2016
  • In 2018 he won an Oscar for his animated short film "Dear Basketball" which also earned him a Sport's Emmy and Annie Award
  • Kobe tragically passed in a helocopter accident along with his daughter Gianna and 6 others
